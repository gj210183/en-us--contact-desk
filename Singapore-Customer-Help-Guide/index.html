Contact Singapore Airlines Customer Service

Need assistance with your booking, flight changes, or general inquiries? Here's how to reach a live representative at Singapore Airlines.

1. Call Singapore Airlines Directly (24/7 Hotline)

The most direct and often the fastest way to get help is by calling Singapore Airlines' main customer service line. Support is available 24/7, so you can call anytime, even in the middle of the night.

Customer Service Number: +1 (888) 829-1117

What you need: Have your booking reference, KrisFlyer number, and travel details ready for faster service.

When to use: Urgent booking changes, cancellations, flight delays, or immediate travel needs.

2. Use the Singapore Airlines Live Chat Feature

If you prefer not to wait on hold, Singapore Airlines' live chat is a fantastic option. It's ideal for quick questions about baggage allowance or seat selection.

How to access: Go to the official Singapore Airlines website or open the Fly Singapore Airlines app, navigate to the “Help” or “Contact Us” section, and start a chat session.

Best for: Quick questions, minor booking adjustments, and when you can’t make a call.

3. Email Singapore Airlines Customer Support

For non-urgent concerns or when you need to send documents (like refund requests or medical certificates), email is ideal.

How to use: Fill out the contact form on the Singapore Airlines website or email through their official support address.

Response time: Usually within a few business days.
